# Stats Draft

## Base stats

### Max HP

Maximum health.

### HP Regeneration

Health restored over time.

### Damage

General damage multiplier.

### Attack Speed

How often weapons attack.

### Range

Weapon reach or projectile travel distance.

### Projectile Speed

How fast projectiles travel.

### Crit Chance

Chance to critically hit.

### Crit Damage

Critical hit damage multiplier.

### Armor

Reduces incoming damage.

### Dodge

Chance to avoid damage.

### Movement Speed

Player movement speed.

### Luck

General chance stat for rewards, drops, and rare outcomes.

### Pickup Range

How far the player collects pickups.

## Economy stats

### XP Gain

Increases experience gained.

### Coin Gain

Increases coins gained.

### Shop Discount

Reduces shop prices.

### Reroll Cost

Changes reroll cost scaling.

## Portal stats

### Portal Luck

Improves rare, beneficial, or high-value portal outcomes.

### Portal Frequency

Increases how often portals appear.

### Portal Instability

Increases extreme portal outcomes. More danger, more reward.

### Portal Reward Multiplier

Improves portal rewards.

### Corruption

A dangerous run modifier that some builds can use as fuel.

## Status stats

### Burn Damage

Improves burn effects.

### Poison Damage

Improves poison effects.

### Bleed Damage

Improves bleed effects.

### Fear Chance

Chance to fear enemies.

### Frost Power

Improves slowing or freezing effects.

## Design note

Stats should support strange build paths, not only linear power increases.
