# Demo Characters

This document defines the first demo character roster.

The demo roster should stay focused and readable. Each character needs a clear fantasy, a starting weapon, a weapon family, a strong passive, one clear downside, and a preferred build direction.

## Character Design Rules

Each character has:

- Starting weapon
- Weapon family
- Passive
- Downside
- Preferred portal types
- Clear build identity

Characters should change how the run feels immediately. They should not be simple stat skins.

---

# 1. The Gunslinger

## Role

Simple ranged direct-damage character.

Best first character for new players.

## Fantasy

Clean bullets, crits, piercing shots, and priority-target killing.

## Starting Weapon

**Heavy Pistol**

Deagle-style weapon. Slow, strong single shots.

## Weapon Family

**Gunslinger Set**

### Weapons

1. Heavy Pistol
2. SMG
3. Shotgun
4. Revolver
5. Assault Rifle
6. Sniper Rifle

## Passive

**Priority Target**

The Gunslinger deals bonus damage to elites, bosses, and the strongest enemy currently in range.

Demo version:

- +20% damage against elites and bosses.

## Downside

**Weak Status Control**

Status effects are weaker.

Demo version:

- -25% status effect power.

## Preferred Portal Types

- Boss Portal
- Greed Portal

## Identity

> I shoot the biggest threat until it disappears.

# 2. The Harvester

## Role

Kill-scaling character.

Starts weaker, but becomes stronger the longer he farms kills.

## Fantasy

I start weak, but every kill feeds the run.

## Starting Weapon

**Scrap Pistol**

## Weapon Family

**Harvester Set**

### Weapons

1. Scrap Pistol
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +1 ranged damage.

2. Bone Knife
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +1 melee damage.

3. Heart Collector
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +1 max HP.

4. Rusted SMG
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +1% attack speed.

5. Grave Rifle
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +1 range.

6. Butcher Tool
   - Every X kills with this weapon: +bonus damage.

## Passive

**Efficient Harvest**

Harvester weapons need fewer kills to gain their kill-scaling bonuses.

Demo version:

- Harvester weapon milestones require 20% fewer kills.

## Downside

**Weak Start**

Demo version:

- -15% starting damage.

## Preferred Portal Types

- Swarm Portal
- Greed Portal

## Identity

> Feed the weapons, survive the weak start, snowball out of control.

# 3. The Demon Lord

## Role

Hellfire destruction character.

High offensive power, no sustain.

## Fantasy

I turn the arena into hell, but I cannot heal through my own arrogance.

## Starting Weapon

**Hellfire Scepter**

## Weapon Family

**Hellfire Set**

### Weapons

1. Hellfire Scepter
   - Shoots demonic fire bolts.

2. Demon Flame Orb
   - Launches slow fire orbs that explode on impact.

3. Infernal Breath
   - Short-range cone of hellfire.

4. Flame Halo
   - Fire orbs circle around the Demon Lord and burn enemies on contact.

5. Hell Chain
   - Fire jumps between burning enemies.

6. Demon Crown
   - Empowers Hellfire weapons when many enemies are burning.

## Passive

**Infernal Flame**

Burn effects are stronger and can become Hellfire.

Hellfire:

- Stronger Burn that deals more damage and spreads better.

## Downside

No lifesteal.

No health regeneration.

## Preferred Portal Types

- Swarm Portal
- Greed Portal

## Identity

> I do not survive by healing. I survive by burning everything before it reaches me.

# 5. The Devil

## Role

Tank / endurance character.

Very hard to kill, very low direct damage.

Wins through long fights and Devil's Debt.

## Fantasy

I do not kill fast.

I outlast everything.

The longer enemies fight me, the more doomed they become.

## Starting Weapon

**Devil's Contract**

## Weapon Family

**Devil Set**

### Weapons

1. Devil's Contract
   - Marks enemies with Devil's Debt.

2. Horned Shield
   - Blocks or reduces damage and applies Debt to attackers.

3. Sin Chain
   - Chains nearby enemies and slowly drains them.

4. Infernal Plate
   - Greatly improves survivability but lowers direct damage.

5. Debt Collector
   - Enemies with Devil's Debt take percent max HP damage when hit.

6. Throne of Sin
   - The longer you survive under pressure, the more Debt spreads.

## Core Mechanic

**Devil's Debt**

Enemies that hit The Devil, stay close to him, or are marked by Devil weapons receive Debt.

Debt can deal percent max HP damage over time or trigger bonus percent damage when the enemy is hit.

## Passive

**Unbreakable Pact**

The Devil gains high survivability and Devil's Debt effects are stronger.

## Downside

Very low direct weapon damage.

Demo version:

- -40% direct weapon damage.

## Preferred Portal Types

- Boss Portal
- Trade Portal
- Greed Portal

## Identity

> You tried to kill me. Now you owe me.

# 6. The Sand Lord

## Role

Arena-control character.

Controls enemy movement, weakens enemies with Erosion, and wins through sandstorms.

## Fantasy

Sand is part of his soul.

He controls dust, dunes, erosion, and tornadoes like an extension of himself.

## Starting Weapon

**Sand Staff**

## Weapon Family

**Sand Set**

### Weapons

1. Sand Staff
   - Shoots sand bolts or small sand swirls. Applies Erosion.

2. Dune Spiral
   - Creates small spiral winds that pull enemies slightly and apply Erosion.

3. Dust Cloak
   - A cloud of cursed dust follows the Sand Lord. Enemies near him gain Erosion over time.

4. Tornado Totem
   - Places small tornadoes near enemies. Tornadoes pull and damage enemies over time.

5. Shifting Sands
   - Sand waves move outward from the Sand Lord. Enemies hit are slowed and eroded.

6. Sandstorm Idol
   - Creates large sandstorm zones. Enemies inside are slowed, eroded, slightly pulled, and damaged over time.

## Core Mechanic

**Erosion**

Enemies affected by Sand lose armor or defense for a short time.

Repeated Sand hits increase the Erosion effect.

## Passive

**Soul of Sand**

Sand effects last longer.

Erosion stacks faster.

Demo version:

- +25% Sand effect duration
- +20% Erosion application speed

## Downside

Low direct damage.

Demo version:

- -20% direct damage.

## Preferred Portal Types

- Swarm Portal
- Greed Portal
- Mutation Portal

## Identity

> I do not chase enemies. I reshape the arena until enemies die inside my storm.
